Comparison AI Image Generation May 2026

Midjourney vs GPT Image 2 vs Ideogram

I tested 10 scenarios across UI design, logos, portraits, and text rendering. Here's which AI image generator actually delivers for designers in 2026.

The Contenders

Midjourney

$10-30/month

The established leader. Known for stunning aesthetics and artistic flair. Operates through Discord with the /imagine command.

GPT Image 2

$20/month (ChatGPT Pro)

OpenAI's latest. Exceptional at following complex prompts and maintaining consistency. Integrated with ChatGPT's ecosystem.

Ideogram

Free / $8-16/month

The text rendering champion. Handles typography in images far better than competitors. Rapidly improving across all metrics.

Tested Scenarios

Scenario 01

UI Design Mockup

Midjourney
Average

Generates beautiful but often unrealistic UI. Buttons, text, and layouts frequently look artistic rather than functional. Hard to get pixel-perfect results.

GPT Image 2
Excellent

Best at generating usable UI mockups. Accurately renders buttons, navigation, text hierarchy. Can create both mobile and desktop interfaces.

Ideogram
Good

Surprisingly capable at UI. Better than expected for app screenshots and interface concepts. Limited control over exact layouts.

Scenario 02

Logo Design

Midjourney
Good

Creates artistic logo concepts but struggles with clean, scalable vector-style logos. Results often look like illustrations rather than brand marks.

GPT Image 2
Excellent

Impressive logo generation. Understands brand aesthetics, creates clean marks, and can match specific style requirements from prompts.

Ideogram
Good

Good for text-based logos especially. The text rendering advantage helps with wordmarks. Icon-style logos are decent but not exceptional.

Scenario 03

Portrait Photography

Midjourney
Excellent

Produces stunning, magazine-quality portraits with beautiful lighting and atmosphere. Often indistinguishable from real photography.

GPT Image 2
Excellent

Excellent photorealism. Particularly strong at maintaining consistent faces across variations. Great for character consistency in campaigns.

Ideogram
Average

Improving but still behind. Faces can look slightly artificial. Best for stylized portraits rather than realistic photography.

Scenario 04

Text in Images

Midjourney
Poor

Text rendering is notoriously unreliable. Generates gibberish most of the time. Essentially unusable for any text-dependent designs.

GPT Image 2
Average

Getting better but still inconsistent. Short words work better. Complex typography or long text often fails or has errors.

Ideogram
Excellent

The clear winner for text. Can reliably render quotes, posters, merchandise designs with accurate typography. Magic Prompt helps too.

Scenario 05

E-commerce Product Photos

Midjourney
Good

Beautiful lifestyle shots but product details can be inaccurate. Good for inspiration, less ideal for final product images.

GPT Image 2
Excellent

Best for product visualization. Can place products in scenes accurately, create consistent backgrounds, and maintain product fidelity.

Ideogram
Good

Solid performer. Good for creating product lifestyle contexts. Text rendering advantage helps with labels and packaging mockups.

Scenario 06

Blog Graphics & Social Media

Midjourney
Good

Beautiful for hero images and abstract visuals. The aesthetic is Instagram-worthy. Requires more editing to add text overlays.

GPT Image 2
Excellent

Versatile for all social formats. Understands composition for different aspect ratios. Great for illustrated and photographic content.

Ideogram
Excellent

Built for this use case. Templates, posters, and social graphics with text work exceptionally well. Great for marketing teams.

My Verdict

The Best AI Image Generator for Designers

For UI/Product Designers: GPT Image 2 is the clear winner. It's the only tool that reliably produces usable UI mockups, product designs, and precise visual elements.

For Brand/Marketing Designers: Ideogram excels when text rendering matters, while Midjourney leads for pure aesthetic quality.

For Everyone: Use multiple tools. Each has strengths. The "best" tool depends entirely on your specific use case.

Recommendations by Use Case

Best for UI/UX Design

GPT Image 2

Best for Art Direction

Midjourney

Best for Marketing

Ideogram

Ideogram

Most generous free tier. Great for teams on a budget who need quick turnaround on text-heavy designs.

Final Thoughts

The AI image generation space is evolving rapidly. What was impossible six months ago is routine today. My recommendation: start with GPT Image 2 for design work, use Ideogram when you need text, and keep Midjourney in your toolkit for artistic projects.

None of these tools replace skilled designers—they make designers faster and more productive. The combination of AI generation plus human refinement is where the real value lies.
