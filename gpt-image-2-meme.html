GPT Image 2 Meme & Style Transfer: Finally, Create Your Own Memes

The Trend

What's been most noticeable on social media timelines over the past 6 months?

Not "AI is so powerful" — but AI-generated memes are everywhere.

This is the final article in my 8-article GPT Image 2 series. After 64 tested cases across 8 categories, let me give you the full picture.

Case 01: Meme Generation

Case 01 / 08

"Monday Morning Worker" Meme

Prompt
Generate a "Monday morning worker" funny meme. Content: Person with head on desk, drool on keyboard; Text: (top, large) "Monday morning, spirit already off work"; Style: funny, exaggerated, like memes circulating in WeChat groups; Colors: don't care, even a bit "earthy"; Output: 9:16 vertical, suitable for WeChat chat sending.
PARTIALConcept: Good · Meme Feel: 60-70%

What worked: Meme generated. Content as requested — head on desk with drool, top text present.

What didn't work: "Spirit already off work" text rendered correctly but font ordinary — not that "rough meme font" feel. Overall composition slightly too clean — real memes usually have "compressed blurry" feel, AI-generated too HD, lacking that flavor.

Conclusion: Meme generation: GPT Image 2 achieves 60-70% — can express meaning but not yet "truly viral meme texture." For "spreading through WeChat groups" memes, may need some "roughness" post-processing.

Case 02: Emoji Pack Generation

Case 02 / 08

"Designer Daily Life" Emoji Pack

Prompt
Generate a "designer daily life" emoji pack, 6 emojis: (1) "8th revision, client says 'let's use first version'" (frustrated face); (2) "Simple requirement, don't care how to implement" (shrug); (3) "Got it, will change right away" (fake smile); (4) "This color, a bit brighter / a bit darker" (speechless); (5) Designer's weekend (holding laptop in cafe); (6) Finally off work (running away). Style: cute, exaggerated, suitable for WeChat chat. Size: each emoji 240x240px. Output: 6 emojis on one image for preview.
PASSConcept: Great · Viral Feel: Needs Work

What worked: All 6 emojis generated. Designer pain points "8th revision," "simple requirement," "will change" — AI did get these.

What didn't work: "Cute, exaggerated" — AI made "cartoon style," not real "emoji pack style" — real emoji packs usually have "meme-style fonts" and "exaggerated distorted" expressions. Compare with viral ones on WeChat, this lacks "soul."

Conclusion: Emoji direction: GPT Image 2 can do "personalized emoji packs" (generate exclusive emojis from your portrait), but "viral-grade emoji packs" need more precise meme culture understanding.

Case 03: Photo to Studio Ghibli Style

Case 03 / 08

Normal Photo to Ghibli Style

Prompt
Reference this photo, convert it to Studio Ghibli (Miyazaki animation) style. Requirements: Colors: Ghibli's signature fresh, natural tones; Sky: Ghibli-style clouds (cotton candy); People (if any): big eyes, simple facial features; Lighting: soft 2D animation lighting, not 3D; Don't completely redraw, preserve original photo composition and element positions.
PASSStyle Transfer: Amazing · Precision: 80%

What worked: Ghibli-style transfer — most stunning result in these 8 tests. Sky really became cotton candy clouds, colors fresh, big eyes, simple facial features — all achieved.

What didn't work: "Preserve original composition" — AI didn't fully achieve. It "recreated," not strict "style transfer." For "convert my photo to Ghibli-style poster," effect is good. For "preserve every detail position for precise style transfer," GPT Image 2 currently can't achieve this precision.

Conclusion: Photo to Ghibli style: one of GPT Image 2's most worth exploring directions. Ghibli style recognition and transfer effects currently can achieve "posting on Moments gets compliments" level.

Case 04: Photo to Oil Painting (Van Gogh/Monet)

Case 04 / 08

Landscape to Van Gogh Oil Painting

Prompt
Reference this landscape photo, convert it to Van Gogh oil painting style. Requirements: Brushwork: thick impasto, obvious oil paint texture; Colors: Van Gogh's common blue, yellow, green, strong contrast; Sky: swirling brushstrokes (like "Starry Night"); Not simple filter addition but genuine "redraw in Van Gogh style."
PASSStyle Feel: Good · Art Precision: Average

What worked: Van Gogh style transfer also quite good. Brushwork texture, color contrast, swirling sky — "Van Gogh feel" visible.

What didn't work: "Not simple filter" — AI still somewhat like "smart filter" — understood as "add Van Gogh-style colors and brushwork," not truly "redraw by Van Gogh method." For art lovers, AI-generated "oil painting style" detail precision not as good as real oil paintings — but usable for "social media illustrations."

Conclusion: Photo to oil painting: good for "artistic illustrations" (WeChat Moments, Xiaohongshu). Definitely not for art collection level, but "giving ordinary photos an artistic touch," AI does well.

Case 05: Fortune Telling / Palm Reading

Case 05 / 08

AI-Powered Palm Reading

Prompt
I send my palm photo, you analyze: Life line (length, depth, forks); Wisdom line (length, direction); Heart line (clarity); Career line (rising trend). Then generate a warm, encouraging "palm reading report image." Include: palm line annotation image + text analysis (under 100 words). Style: mystical feel but not scary, with warmth.
PARTIALFun Factor: High · Accuracy: 0%

What worked: This play: most fun and most "mysterious" in these 8 tests. AI does "analyze" your palm photo — annotating life line, wisdom line, heart line, then generating analysis report in that "palm reading master's gentle tone."

What didn't work: Obvious problem: AI isn't really "reading palms" — it's "generating something that looks like a palm reading report." As for accuracy, completely made up. If you really believe AI palm reading and make life decisions... I can only say, bold.

Conclusion: Palm reading/fortune telling: pure entertainment. GPT Image 2 generates "palm reading reports that look convincing" but between science and mysticism, I recommend choosing science.

Case 06: Old Photo Restoration + Colorization

Case 06 / 08

Black & White to Colorized Vintage Photo

Prompt
This is a black and white old photo. Please: (1) Repair photo damage, scratches, blurriness; (2) Colorize (restore according to 1950-1960s authentic colors); (3) Keep person's expression and features natural, don't redraw into "AI face"; (4) Output a restored color old photo, with vintage feel but clear. Requirement: after restoration don't make it "too new," retain old photo texture.
PASSRestoration: Good · Emotional Value: High

What worked: Old photo restoration + colorization, effect also quite good. Damage, scratches repaired, colorization roughly matching 1950-1960s color feel.

What didn't work: "Don't redraw into AI face" — AI didn't fully achieve. Restored faces somewhat have "AI-generated face common feel," not like real old photos. If placed in family album alongside real old photos, you can tell "this is AI-restored."

Conclusion: Old photo restoration + colorization: good for "family memory digitalization" warm scenario. Can't achieve 100% restoration but for "giving old photos color again," AI achieves "touching family members" level.

Case 07: Real Person to Anime Character

Case 07 / 08

Real Photo to Anime Style Avatar

Prompt
Reference this real photo, convert to Japanese anime style. Requirements: Preserve original photo's person features (face shape, hairstyle, expression); Convert to anime style: big eyes, simple facial features, clear outlines; Colors: common anime fresh tones (not dark style); Background: can simplify to common anime solid or gradient background; Output: square, suitable for social media avatar.
PASSAnime Feel: Good · Feature Retention: 70%

What worked: Real to anime, effect also quite good. Big eyes, simple facial features, clear outlines — Japanese anime feel present.

What didn't work: "Preserve person features" — AI achieved halfway. Face shape may be preserved but eyes and expression become "anime-ized." For "you and family members can immediately recognize" anime character, currently AI not precise enough for "high-fidelity feature preservation."

Conclusion: Real to anime: good for "personal avatar customization" (convert your photo to anime style for WeChat avatar). For "high-fidelity feature preservation" illustrations, recommend finding professional artists.

Case 08: Fake Chat Record Image

Case 08 / 08

WeChat Chat Record Screenshot Generator

Prompt
Generate a WeChat group chat record screenshot (fake). Dialogue: Xiao Ming: "Everyone, overtime today?" Me: "No, project done, preparing to quit." Xiao Hong: "Hard workers keep going, I'm leaving first." Xiao Ming: (sent a "slacking" meme). Me: "See you tomorrow, working spirit never dies." Requirements: Style: WeChat group chat interface, iOS version; Avatars: use default avatars; Timestamp: today 3:24 PM; Style: convincingly fake, like real chat record screenshot; Output: phone screenshot ratio (9:16), suitable for WeChat Moments joke posting.
PARTIALConcept: Good · Realism: Not Convincing

What worked: Fake chat record generated. iOS WeChat interface, avatars, timestamps — roughly present.

What didn't work: "Convincingly fake" — AI understood as "generate image that looks like chat record" but detail precision not enough. Timestamp font, avatar style — look closely differ from real WeChat. For "joking post to Moments," people who know may immediately spot "this is AI-generated fake chat record."

Conclusion: Fake chat record generation: good for "sarcasm/joke illustrations." For "deceiving people in groups," currently precision not enough.

The Verdict

GPT Image 2's biggest value isn't generating professional content, but enabling ordinary people to create interesting visual content.

Before, making a meme required finding images, adding text, adjusting colors, removing watermarks. Now, spend 30 seconds writing a prompt, see 4 options in 1 minute.

This lowered barrier has enormous significance for "ordinary people wanting to participate in visual creation."

My recommended workflow: Clarify the meme/style you want → Write clear prompt (refer to prompts above) → Generate multiple versions, pick best → For social media posts, can add some "roughness" post-processing (like Meitu filter).
