Frequently Asked Questions

Can GPT Image 2 replace UI designers?
GPT Image 2 excels at design exploration and mood boards but struggles with pixel-perfect, production-ready UI. Best used as a brainstorming tool, not a replacement for Figma or professional designers.
What UI elements does GPT Image 2 do best?
Based on our 8-scenario test: Onboarding flows (4.8/5), Auth screens (4.6/5), Empty states (4.4/5), and Product grids (4.3/5) are GPT Image 2's strongest areas.
How to write effective prompts for UI generation?
Include: 1) Specific UI type (mobile/web), 2) Design language (iOS/Material), 3) Color theme, 4) Key elements needed, 5) Quality descriptors. The more specific, the better the results.
Is GPT Image 2 free to use?
GPT Image 2 is available through ChatGPT Pro at $20/month. It's not on the free tier. For free alternatives with UI capabilities, consider Ideogram.
